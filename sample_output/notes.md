### Submission Process (Demonstration Steps)
1.  **Join Hackathon:** Click "Join Hackathon" on the Kaggle project page and accept the terms.
2.  **Navigate to Submissions:** After joining, you'll see "View Write-ups" or similar.
3.  **Create New Write-up:** Click "New Write-up."
4.  **Project Details:**
    *   **Title:** Enter the project title.
    *   **Subtitle:** Add a subtitle.
    *   **Cover Image:** Edit image and upload a suitable cover image.
    *   **Select Track:** Choose one of the four project tracks (e.g., "Agents for Business").
5.  **Media Gallery:**
    *   **Add Image:** Upload a project image.
    *   **Add Video:** Provide the YouTube URL for your project video.
6.  **Product Description/Write-up:** Copy and paste your detailed project description into the provided text area.
7.  **Attachments (Project Link):**
    *   Click "Add Link."
    *   Enter your GitHub URL.
    *   Add a title for the link (e.g., "GitHub Repository").
    *   Click "Insert."
8.  **Review Checklist:** Ensure all mandatory items (video, images, links) are included.
9.  **Preview:** Click "Preview" to see how your submission will appear. Verify all sections (problem statement, architecture, use cases, links) are present and correct.
10. **Submit:** Once satisfied, click "Submit."
